Therapy

Are you looking for individual, family,
and/or couples’ therapy?

I focus on various aspects of therapy which may range from depression, anxiety, addressing past trauma, increasing attachment, relational challenges, and/or everything in between? Services are client-centered to ensure that you are getting what you need. There will be a plan developed specifically for your needs, and I will be transparent and honest at each stage of therapy.

I utilize Play therapy, Attachment dynamics, Cognitive based therapy, Family Systems and other forms of therapy. I work with adults and children of all ages to assist each family member in achieving self-empowerment and to optimize their ability to function within the family and in today’s society.
